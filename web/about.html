About Montfort Brothers Uganda

Rooted in faith. United in service. Empowering through education.

Our Mission

To nurture compassionate leaders through holistic education, spiritual formation, and outreach to the marginalized.

Faith & Formation

We guide young hearts through prayer, reflection, and the legacy of St. Louis de Montfort.

Education & Empowerment

Our schools foster excellence, creativity, and lifelong learning rooted in values.

Service & Outreach

We serve communities with love — from pastoral care to youth mentorship and social action.

Our Heritage

Inspired by the life and teachings of St. Louis-Marie Grignion de Montfort.

St. Montfort’s Legacy

His motto “God Alone” continues to guide our Brothers in faith, humility, and service.

Our Journey in Uganda

From Mbarara to Mpala, our presence grows through education, formation, and community engagement.

Core Values

  • Faith: Anchored in prayer and spiritual growth
  • Unity: Living our motto “Unite for Peace” in every action
  • Compassion: Serving with love and dignity
  • Excellence: Pursuing knowledge and creativity
  • Community: Building bridges across cultures and generations
